About

Hands-on from CAD to finished build

I'm Leon Xu, a mechanical engineering student at the University of Michigan focused on design, simulation, and fabrication. I've been 3D printing since 2019, shipped a production CAD module at a commercial simulation-software company, and led controls for a FIRST Hall of Fame robotics team.

What I enjoy most is owning the full pipeline — defining requirements, designing in CAD, validating with simulation, then actually machining, printing, welding, and wiring the thing together and testing it. I'm now pursuing a Business minor to pair that engineering depth with product and commercial thinking.
